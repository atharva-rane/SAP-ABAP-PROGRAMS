*Loop Operation - 1. Concatinate Operation.
*                 2. Calculate number of lines.
*                 3. Display Internal Table

       LOOP At gt_emp INTO gs_emp.

         "1. Concatinate Operation
         CONCATENATE gs_emp-fname gs_emp-lname INTO gs_emp-full_name SEPARATED BY space.


         "2. Calculate total number of lines in iternal table. [create a variable named gs_lines]
         DESCRIBE TABLE gt_emp LINES gs_lines.


         "3. Display Internal Table
         write : /5 gs_emp-emp_id, 15 gs_emp-full_name, 40 gs_emp-emp_dept, 55 gs_emp-ctc LEFT-JUSTIFIED.
         ULINE.

        ENDLOOP.


        write : 'Total number of lines in the internal table: ', gs_lines.
        ULINE.
